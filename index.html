F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does your school admit Muslim pupils only?

    Not really! Even though our school has an Islamic foundation, we accept pupils from different beliefs.

  • Do you teach following the standard curriculum?

    Yes! Our school teaches content following the measures provided by the Ministry of Education and Sports together with the National Curriculum Development Centre.

  • Do you give out bursaries at your school?

    Yes! We have a bursary programme for specific categories of pupils like the talented ones and the disadvantaged ones.

  • Do you allow pupils from different religions to worship their God?

    We respect all doctrines and give chance to all pupils to praise their God on the specified days of worship.

  • Does your school handle children of all ages?

    We have an infant section for very young children, lower primary section for grown children and upper primary section for mature children.

  • Can I bring my child to your school now?

    Registration is already in progress at the school premises. Get in touch with the school administration using the contacts provided on this website.
